Our Story

Bujuko Miracle Park & Gardens was established in 1998 as a passion project to preserve and showcase the rich botanical heritage of Uganda. Located in the heart of BUJUUKO TOWN, our park spans over 5 acres of carefully curated natural landscapes.

What started as a small collection of native plants has grown into a thriving sanctuary that houses over 80 species of indigenous flora, medicinal herbs, and rare trees. Our mission is to connect people with nature and promote environmental conservation through education and peaceful immersion.
